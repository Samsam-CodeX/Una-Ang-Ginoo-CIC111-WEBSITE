Una ang Ginoo

The motto "Una ang Ginoo" is the core principle of Tangub City, meaning "God First," which guides all city government decisions and community life, establishing a moral and spiritual foundation for governance based on integrity and service.

Light of the World

The phrase "The Light of the World" in Tangub City Global College (TCGC) is the institution's core aspiration to produce God-centered, globally competitive graduates who will act as beacons of integrity, compassion, and excellence by using their knowledge to serve their communities and positively impact the world.
