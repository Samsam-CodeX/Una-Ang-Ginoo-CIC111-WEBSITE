Una ang Ginoo

The motto "Una ang Ginoo" is the core principle of Tangub City, meaning "God First," which guides all city government decisions and community life, establishing a moral and spiritual foundation for governance based on integrity and service.

Light of the World

The phrase "The Light of the World" in Tangub City Global College (TCGC) is the institution's core aspiration to produce God-centered, globally competitive graduates who will act as beacons of integrity, compassion, and excellence by using their knowledge to serve their communities and positively impact the world.

My Personal Reflection

Faith | Integrity | Responsible Digital Citizenship

I. Faith

“I can do all things through Christ who strengthens me.” - Philippians 4:13

My faith is my strength when life feels impossible. When I say "I can do all things," it is a promise rooted in Christ, meaning I have the power for the next hard step, relying not on my own willpower, but on the feeling of being completely supported by God. I know I don't have to carry the whole heavy load alone. This quiet certainty lets me face confusing moments, trusting that the strength I need will always show up when I need it most. It is the reliable reason I keep moving forward.

II. Integrity

This strength I find in my faith is what empowers me to live with integrity. For me, integrity means the daily, consistent commitment to aligning my actions with my beliefs, even when no one is watching. As a student, this requires choosing honest effort in academics over shortcuts and being reliable to my friends. My reflection is simple: Am I taking the easy way, or the honest way? I strive to use my faith to ensure my decisions build a solid foundation of trust and respect every day.

III. Responsible Digital Citizenship

Because I must be consistent, I treat the internet just like my real-world community. Being a good digital citizen means my faith and integrity rules still apply online. Every single post, comment, or picture I share affects real people. So, I promise to use technology to build others up and to learn, not to hurt or spread bad feelings. My biggest test of integrity online is pausing to practice empathy before I hit 'send,' making sure my actions reflect the honest, positive person I aim to be.

Lux Mundi: Becoming a True Light of the World

Living the Core Values of TCGC College: Integrity, Compassion, Excellence

The Call to Be Light

The title Lux Mundi is a promise—a commitment to bring clarity, warmth, and ethical guidance to every sphere of life. To truly embody this spirit, we must integrate the foundational values instilled by TCGC College into our daily habits and interactions. This guide offers practical steps to make these values shine.

Putting TCGC Core Values Into Action

Our three core values are not just statements; they are action guides. Here is how we apply them to shine brightly:

Core Value 1: Integrity

Definition: The adherence to moral and ethical principles; the state of being whole and undivided in character.

  1. Own Your Mistakes: When something goes wrong, immediately take responsibility. Integrity means admitting fault and focusing on repair, not denial.
  2. Uphold Academic Honesty: Always cite your sources, never plagiarize, and complete tests and assignments based solely on your own work. Your diploma should reflect your true effort.
  3. Follow Through: Do what you said you would do, even when it’s inconvenient or no one is watching. Your private actions must align with your public promises.

Core Value 2: Compassion

Definition: Sympathetic pity and concern for the sufferings or misfortunes of others, combined with a desire to help.

  1. Listen Actively: When a classmate or peer shares a struggle, put away distractions and listen without interrupting or preparing your own response. Empathy begins with attention.
  2. Practice Online Kindness: Avoid harsh criticism, ridicule, or dismissive comments in social media or group chats. Assume the best intent and respond with patience.
  3. Be an Inclusive Presence: Actively seek out those who are marginalized or quiet. A truly compassionate environment ensures that everyone feels seen and valued.

Core Value 3: Excellence

Definition: The quality of being outstanding or extremely good; striving for the highest possible standards in all endeavors.

  1. Focus on Effort, Not Just Grade: Excellence is the continuous commitment to giving your best effort in every subject and project, regardless of the difficulty.
  2. Embrace Constructive Criticism: View feedback from instructors and peers as a tool for growth, not a personal attack. Use it to continuously refine your skills and character.
  3. Go Beyond the Minimum: Look for opportunities to enhance your learning, whether through extra reading, joining academic clubs, or mentoring others. A light that grows brighter through constant learning.

Sustaining the Light

Being a Lux Mundi is a constant choice. It requires daily commitment to reflection and action, ensuring that the light you carry is steady, warm, and guides others toward truth and goodness. By living Integrity, Compassion, and Excellence every day, you fulfill the highest purpose of the TCGC College community.
